The Packaging INFLECTION POINT

Five forces are converging on FMCG packaging. None of them are optional.

The packaging industry is facing its most significant shift in decades. For premium brands like Fever-Tree, understanding these forces is not a technical exercise. It is a commercial imperative.

GS1 Sunrise 2027

Every barcode on every SKU must migrate to 2D by December 2027. POS systems across 48+ countries. Not a label update. A packaging event.

Extended Producer Responsibility

UK EPR is live. Per unit fees now penalise overproduction. Brands that print to forecast rather than demand carry a direct cost penalty.

Short Run Economics

Seasonal variants, NPD testing, retailer exclusives. The commercial model demands flexibility that long run printing was never designed for.

Personalisation & Engagement

QR to experience journeys, provenance storytelling, unique codes per pack. Consumers expect packaging to be a digital touchpoint, not dead print.

Sustainability & Waste

Obsolete stock, makeready waste, overruns. The environmental and financial cost of inflexible packaging is no longer acceptable.

These are not technology trends. They are commercial realities. The brands that respond first will set the standard their competitors have to follow.
